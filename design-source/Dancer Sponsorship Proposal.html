Overview

The No Boundaries Dance Foundation, Inc. is seeking partnership with the Moreno Valley City Council to support a targeted youth enrichment initiative through the arts. This proposal outlines an opportunity for each council member to sponsor one dancer from their district, providing access to high-quality dance education, mentorship, and personal development.

Our goal is simple: remove financial barriers and give deserving youth the opportunity to grow through structured training, discipline, and creative expression.

Program Structure

Each Sponsored Dancer Receives:

  • Weekly dance classes (multiple styles)
  • Personalized coaching and skill development
  • Performance opportunities
  • Mentorship: confidence, discipline, goal setting

Funds Directly Cover:

  • Tuition and training expenses
  • Coaching and instructional support
  • Program materials
  • Performance preparation

Community Impact

This program is designed to serve youth who may not otherwise have access to structured arts education. Through this initiative, dancers will:

  • Build confidence and self-esteem
  • Develop discipline and commitment
  • Improve physical and mental well-being
  • Engage in a positive, structured environment
  • Represent their community through performance

Beyond the individual dancer, this investment strengthens families and contributes to a more connected, engaged community.

Selection Process

Each council member will have the opportunity to collaborate with the No Boundaries Dance Foundation to identify a deserving participant from their district. Selection will prioritize:

  • Financial need
  • Commitment and interest in dance
  • Potential for growth and positive community impact
Accountability & Reporting

The No Boundaries Dance Foundation will provide transparent reporting throughout the program:

  • Progress updates on each sponsored dancer
  • Program participation summaries
  • End-of-program impact report

Council members will have the opportunity to attend performances and see the direct results of their investment.

Sponsor Recognition & Benefits

All sponsoring council members will receive the following recognition:

  • Name posted in weekly digital newsletter through August 30th — 750 homes reached weekly
  • Name listed in the Annual Dance Concert "Create", June 26th & 27th — approx. 1,800 in attendance
  • Two complimentary tickets to the Annual Dance Concert "Create," June 26th and June 27th

About No Boundaries Dance Foundation

The No Boundaries Dance Foundation, Inc. is dedicated to expanding access to dance education for youth in our community. Through scholarships, sponsorships, and outreach programs, we strive to create opportunities that inspire growth, confidence, and lifelong skills.

Closing

This is a focused, tangible way to impact five young lives — one from each district — while strengthening the cultural and developmental opportunities within Moreno Valley.

We welcome the opportunity to partner with the City Council and bring this vision to life. Thank you for your time, consideration, and continued commitment to our community.

Suzi Zuppardo
Director, No Boundaries Dance Foundation, Inc.
