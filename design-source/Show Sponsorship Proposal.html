Overview

The No Boundaries Dance Foundation, Inc. invites the Moreno Valley City Council to partner with us in sponsoring our Annual Dance Concert — a premier community event celebrating youth, arts, and achievement.

This year's production will include three performances across June 26th and 27th, with an anticipated 2,500 attendees from Moreno Valley and surrounding communities. The concert highlights the dedication and growth of our dancers while creating a meaningful experience for families and residents.

Sponsorship Opportunity

We are offering sponsorship opportunities for key elements of the production at $1,000 each. Each of the five City Council members may sponsor a specific area of the production, directly aligning their support with a featured element of the show.

Area 01
Opening Number Sponsor
Kick off the show with a dynamic, high-energy performance that sets the tone for the entire event.
Area 02

Area 03
Lobby Photo Experience
Sponsor the themed photo area — one of the most visible and highly engaged spaces throughout the event.
Area 04

Sponsor Recognition & Benefits

Each sponsoring council member will receive meaningful visibility and engagement opportunities:

  • Recognition in theater lobby signage during all performances
  • Promotion to current clientele through studio communications
  • Social media recognition and features across our platforms
  • Inclusion in a local advertising publication connected to the event
  • Association with a 2,500-attendee community event
  • VIP Seating for sponsor + one guest at each performance
Community Impact

This annual concert is more than a performance — it is a structured opportunity for youth to grow in confidence, discipline, and teamwork. Through this event, dancers:

  • Experience the reward of consistent training and commitment
  • Build self-confidence through performance
  • Engage in a positive and supportive environment
  • Represent the Moreno Valley community with pride

City Council sponsorship directly supports the quality of this experience and reinforces a shared commitment to youth development and the arts.
